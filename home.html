AUTISM IN VIETNAM

"No more ignorance, discrimination, and prejudice."

According to statistics collected by Tuổi Trẻ and VNExpress news:



There are approximately 1 million autistic children in Vietnam and 8 million people get directly affected, estimated by professionals. The majority of the 1 million autistic children in Vietnam haven't received proper diagnosis, thereby not getting enough help, care, treatment, and education that can significantly impact one's happiness.


Symptoms start appearing at the age of 2. The earlier an autistic child is diagnosed and received intervention, the better the life quality of the child later on.





In order to help the autistic children receive earlier treatment and better assimilate to society, we should be educated on the autism spectrum disorder.
